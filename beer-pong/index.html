HOW TO PLAY

Swipe up from the bottom of the screen to throw. Swipe speed sets power, swipe direction aims, a curved swipe adds side-spin, snapping at the end throws flat & hard, a soft release lofts a drop shot.

Makes build your streak: ×1 → ×2 → ×3 → ×5 → ×8. A miss resets it — a rimmed-out shot only halves it.
A cup left touching no others glows purple — an island, worth 3× or more. Tap RE-RACK (2 per run) to pull the remaining cups into a tight pyramid — but it erases islands.
Everything you score is a pot at risk. Run out of balls with cups standing = CRASH — pot gone. Tap CASH OUT between throws to bank it. Clear a rack and all points ahead get ×1.5. Quitting banks nothing.
